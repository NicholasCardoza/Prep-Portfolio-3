ABOUT ME

By trade I am a fabricator and master welder. Using my ability to build and create projects in the movies (staging and props for the Spider-Man movies), large concert rigging, fast food restaurants, (Del Taco, Chipotle, Starbucks, Coffee Bean, and Dave's Hot Chicken), cell phone towers and they're charging stations for commercial, industrial, and military. More recently I have dedicated my time to front end web development. Focusing on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.Switching over to web development is a new and exciting Adventure full of hard work and dedication.

In my free time I love to be outdoors. No matter if it's shooting archery tournaments, riding dirt bikes and jet skiing in the summer. When the snow comes its snowboarding, skiing and snowmobiling during the winter. This time is split between work, new Investments and hobbies.
